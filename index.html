About Me:




Matthew Huberty hails from Northeast Ohio. He grew up in the Youngstown, OH area and graduated high school from Canfield High. He earned a bachelor's degree from Duquesne University, Pittsburgh, PA. He also earned a Master's degree (MBA) in 2008, from Carnegie Mellon University. There, he concentrated in finance, accounting, economics and quantitative analysis. One of his hobbies is in building software and coding websites. A current student at the UC Davis Coding bootcamp, he is sharpening his front-end stack and web development skillset.


Matthew earned certifications as a:

  • CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst),
  • CFP (Certified Financial Planner),
  • FRM (Financial Risk Manager),
  • CPA (Certified Public Accountant).

He currently lives in West Sacramento, CA.
